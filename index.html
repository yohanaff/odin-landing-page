The history of retro games

The first known instance of the term "retro" in terms of gaming came from the online video game store RetroGames.

Some of the most famous retro games

  • Pac-Man

  • Tetris

  • Street Fighter II

  • Super Mario Bros

  • The classic and enormously popular Pac-Man video game came out in Japan on May 21, 1980

  • Tetris is a puzzle video game created by Soviet software engineer Alexey Pajitnov in 1984

  • On Feb. 6, 1991, Street Fighter II was released as an arcade game in North America

  • The first game in the series, Super Mario Bros., released for the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES) in 1985
